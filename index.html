About Me

Let me introduce myself.

I am a music and arts teacher. In music, I have professional experience as a teacher of piano, music theory, keyboard, children's musicalization, vocal preparation and choral conducting (children, young people, adults and seniors).I also worked as a researcher of local popular culture Reis de boi (São Mateus), participating in meetings, workshops and congresses in the area of popular culture with articles published at UFF (UNIVERSIDADE FEDERAL FLUMINENSE - NITEROI RJ), at UFBA (UNIVERSIDADE FEDERAL DA BAHIA SALVADOR ), at (UNIVERSIDADE ESTADUAL DO ESTADO DE PIRINOPOLIS GOIAS) and in the EUROPEAN REVIEW OF ARTISTIC STUDIES in the city of Porto in PORTUGAL. Also published in PERIODICO DA UFES magazine.
